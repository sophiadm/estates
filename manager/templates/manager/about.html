About Eastgate Estates

Who are we?

Eastgate Estates are a medium sized residential property company based in sunny Tottenham in North London. We are professional landlords who own all our own properties, most of which are scattered are North and East London. The company is fully vertically integrated anddevelops, owns, maintains and manages an and wide-ranging porfolio of houses, Including all types of residential properties ranging from studio to three bed flats and we also have some large houses which are let as shared (HMO) accommodation.

Why rent from us

Where we differ from many other landlords is that we regard our tenants as our clients and believe that as they are paying us rent every month they are also entitled to a good service, whenever they have a problem with their accommodation they need us to take care of. We pride orselves in the support delivered to clients, something which the company is always striving to improve. Clients employ our services as they look to find their feet in a new city and environment, as they move across neighbourhoods to be closer to work or that all important station, our high level of customer satisfaction from our service and customer care is reflected in the low turn-over of our client tenants as most people who rent a property from us stay with us for many years, as they appreciate the fact that we keep their home in good condition and quickly attend to any problems
