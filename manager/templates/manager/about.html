About Eastgate Estates

Who are we?

Eastgate Estates was established nearly forty years ago. The company, based in Northumberland Park, owns, manages and maintains a wide range of individual properties available for rent across North and East London. Our friendly team is ‘big enough to cope, small enough to care’. We develop and maintain all types of residential properties ranging from studio to three bed flats and we also have some large houses which are let as shared (HMO) accommodation.

Why rent from us

Eastgate Estates prides itself on the all-round service that we deliver to our client tenants. We know our clients by name and our head office staff will recognise you when you call in to discuss any matter relating to your accommodation. It is this level of personal attention that sets us aside from other companies and makes dealing with Eastgate such a pleasure. We know that things happen from time to time and of course, we are measured on how we respond to matters as they arise. Our own in-house maintenance team is available throughout the week and will respond to any situation in order of priority.

What we offer

The portfolio of rental properties ranges from 1 to 3 bedroomed flats, 1 to 4 bedroomed houses as well as single or double rooms in flat and house shares. Properties in North and East London are well situated for easy access to London’s extensive transport network, perfect for travelling across town for work and pleasure. Each individual property is allocated into an ongoing, planned maintenance programme designed to maintain the condition of your London home.
